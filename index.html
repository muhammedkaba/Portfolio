Party Club

Party Club is a cooperative venue management game where players team up to run a lively party filled with quirky animal customers, each with unique personalities and seating preferences. The main challenge is to strategically seat guests to avoid conflicts, serve drinks efficiently, and manage the venue by repairing equipment and cleaning up. The game features diverse environments-from cozy towns to fiery volcanoes-that add variety and challenge.

Supporting up to four players both online and locally, Party Club emphasizes teamwork and quick coordination. Special customers with unique effects, like skunks that drive others away, add depth to the gameplay. Power-ups such as roller skates for speed and hammers for repairs help players handle the hectic pace. With its charming art style and engaging mechanics, Party Club offers a fun and strategic multiplayer experience.

Devil Can Beat

Devil Can Beat is a rogue-lite rhythm action game where you battle enemies in sync with the music. Combining fast-paced combat and procedural dungeons, each level challenges you to time your attacks and dodges perfectly to the beat. Unlock powerful upgrades, customize your playstyle with unique power-ups, and face intense boss fights. With immersive sound design driving the gameplay, it offers a fresh blend of rhythm and action for players seeking a challenging, musical adventure.
